Italian Sausage Lasagna

Credit: Dotdash Meredith Video Studios

Homemade lasagna takes time and patience but it's well worth it!

Follow this recipe to make the best lasagna in the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound hot italian fennel sausage
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 4 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 12 sheets no-boil lasagna noodles
